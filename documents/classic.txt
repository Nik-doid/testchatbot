
About Us

Company Profile

Classic Tech is a licensed Internet Service Provider (ISP) and Network Service Provider (NSP) established in July 2009. Over the years, it has rapidly expanded its services and gained significant customer trust, becoming one of Nepal's leading ISPs and NSPs. The company operates in 74 districts across Nepal, providing high-speed internet and IPTV services.

Mission

Classic Tech aims to innovate and deliver quality network services that meet the diverse needs of its users. The company is committed to providing reliable and affordable services, thereby empowering the nation.

Vision

The vision of Classic Tech is to offer high-speed and reliable internet and network services, coupled with responsive customer support and value-added services, ensuring complete customer satisfaction.

Goals and Objectives

- Enhance internet availability and accessibility in rural areas.
- Generate employment opportunities both directly and indirectly.
- Promote and develop the interests of ISPs and NSPs.
- Collaborate with national and international organizations to stay updated on technologies and best practices.

Key to Success

Classic Tech attributes its success to:

- Customer service excellence.
- Transparent communication.
- Innovation and efficiency.
- Customer satisfaction.
- Teamwork.
- Community engagement.

FAQ

1. How can I request a new installation?

You can request a new installation through the following methods:

- Open a new ticket via the Classic Tech support app.
- Visit the nearest Classic Tech branch.
- Call the toll-free number: 16600150999.
- Contact customer service at: 9802922304.
- Chat via Facebook: Classic Tech Facebook.
- Visit the website: Classic Tech Support.

2. How do I check my IPTV expiry date?

Your IPTV expiry date is displayed on the homepage of the Classic Tech app. It expires when your internet subscription expires.

3. How can I extend my IPTV account?

IPTV is provided free of cost to existing customers. When your subscription expires, the IPTV service also expires. After renewing your internet subscription, the IPTV service will automatically extend.

4. Why is my IPTV not working?

IPTV operates through the internet. If there is no internet service, IPTV will not work. For other technical issues, you can create a support ticket via the Classic Tech app or contact customer service at 9801006633 or 9802922304.

5. How can I register IPTV-related problems?

To register IPTV-related issues:

- Open the Classic Tech app and create a support ticket.
- Call customer service at 9802922304 or 9801006633.
- Email: care@classic.com.np.

6. What is the Classic Tech app?

The Classic Tech app allows users to access account information, check data usage, register complaints, upgrade packages, recharge, and view offers. It is available for download on:

- App Store
- Google Play Store

7. How can I get support from the Classic Tech app?

To get support:

- Open the Classic Tech app.
- Navigate to the support section.
- Choose the appropriate option to open a ticket, email, or call.

8. What is Service Payback?

Service Payback compensates customers for service outages exceeding 24 hours. To claim, create a ticket via the Classic Tech app or contact customer service at 9801006633 or 9802922304.

9. How can I modify my Wi-Fi username and password?

You can modify your Wi-Fi password but not the username:

- Log in to the Classic Tech app.
- Go to the router settings.
- Edit the Wi-Fi password as needed.

10. What is FUP (Fair Usage Policy)?

FUP is implemented to prevent misuse or excessive use of bandwidth on unlimited plans. It ensures quality internet services for all customers by restricting speeds when data usage exceeds certain thresholds.
