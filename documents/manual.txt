1. INTRODUCTION  
The EcoHome Smart Thermostat Pro learns your schedule, adjusts to weather changes, and can be controlled remotely via smartphone to save energy.  

Key Features:  
- 7-day programmable scheduling  
- Voice control (Alexa/Google Assistant)  
- Energy usage reports  
- Geofencing  

───────────────────────────────────────  
2. INSTALLATION GUIDE  
⚠️ Turn off HVAC power at breaker before installation.  

2.1 What’s in the Box:  
- Thermostat baseplate  
- Screws & anchors  
- Wire labels  
- Quick start guide  

2.2 Wiring:  
Label existing wires before removal. Match to terminals:  
- R: Power  
- C: Common  
- W: Heat  
- Y: Cool  
- G: Fan  

2.3 Mounting:  
1. Attach baseplate to wall.  
2. Connect wires.  
3. Snap thermostat onto baseplate.  

───────────────────────────────────────  
3. BASIC OPERATIONS  
3.1 Manual Control:  
- Tap ▲/▼ to adjust temperature.  
- Press ☀️ for heat mode, ❄️ for cool.  

3.2 Setting a Schedule:  
Menu → Schedule → Set times/temps for each day.  

───────────────────────────────────────  
4. ADVANCED FEATURES  
4.1 Geofencing:  
App → Settings → Enable "Auto-Away" to adjust temps when you leave.  

4.2 Voice Control:  
"Alexa, set living room to 72°"  

───────────────────────────────────────  
5. WI-FI & APP SETUP  
1. Download "EcoHome" app.  
2. Create account.  
3. Select "Add Device" and follow prompts.  

───────────────────────────────────────  
6. TROUBLESHOOTING  
❌ Thermostat won’t turn on:  
- Check breaker & C-wire connection.  

❌ App connection failed:  
- Reboot router.  
- Ensure 2.4GHz Wi-Fi (not 5GHz).  

───────────────────────────────────────  
7. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)  

Q: Does this work with heat pumps?  
A: Yes, but enable "O/B reversal" in settings if needed.  

Q: Why does my screen go blank?  
A: Battery may be low. Check wiring or use USB power during setup.  

Q: Can I control multiple thermostats?  
A: Yes, via app groups (max 4 per home).  

Q: How do I reset to factory settings?  
A: Hold ⚙️ + ❄️ for 10 seconds.
